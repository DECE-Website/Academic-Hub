Undergraduate Programs

Understanding Academic Programs

An academic program is the program to which a student applies and is admitted and from which the student graduates.

Understanding Academic Plans

An academic plan is an area of study within an academic program or career — it represents one of the following: Major programs of study, minor program of study, Certificate or Concentration or specialization.
