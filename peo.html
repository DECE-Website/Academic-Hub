Program Educational Objectives-1 (PEO-1)
LEADERSHIP

Graduates are Electronics Engineers occupying positions requiring highly technical expertise in specialized fields of electronics engineering imbibed with responsible leadership and ethical values.
Program Educational Objectives-2 (PEO-2)
LIFE-LONG LEARNING

Graduates demonstrate life-long learning and continuous professional and career development while practicing their profession
Program Educational Objectives-3 (PEO-3)
SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY

Graduates will demonstrate service for the community and society that helps uplift the lives of the people and will manifest concern for the environment.
