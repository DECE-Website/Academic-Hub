Conference

Seminars, Conferences, and Call for Papers

The academic departments within the different colleges collaborate with the research and professional sectors in their respective fields to promote and exchange new ideas, and share industry trends and experiences in conferences. They meet to discuss and consolidate innovative approaches to improve ways and tools in their areas of expertise. Resolutions in conferences are designed to improve productivity and be responsive to human needs.
