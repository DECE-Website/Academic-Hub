Graduation Special Awards

The Honors and Awards Council recognizes the following Graduation Special Awards at the undergraduate level:

  • Br. Gabriel Connon Award
  • Student Leadership Award
  • Community Development Award
  • Athletics Award
  • Cultural Development Award

These awards are given during the Commencement Exercises. Applicants for these awards undergo the screening process with the following stages: (a) Application; (b) Initial Screening by referendum; and (c) Final Screening.The application period is from Week 5 to Week 8 of every regular term. Application kits are available online. Detailed procedures, requirements, and schedules are included in the kit.Only applicants who obtain a score of at least 85% in the initial screening by referendum will qualify for the final screening stage.During the final screening stage, each applicant shall be interviewed by the members of the Honors and Awards Council and rated by ballot.
