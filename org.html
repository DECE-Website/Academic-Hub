Office of Student Leadership Involvement, Formation and Empowerment

The Office of Student Leadership Involvement, Formation, and Empowerment (Student L.I.F.E.) seek to develop and implement programs that promote self-sustaining development and positive influence towards effective personal and organizational leadership. It takes charge of ensuring smooth and well-balanced implementation of co-curricular and extra-curricular activities of the different student groups under the Student Council and the Council of Student Organizations. It also encourages student involvement and helps orient freshmen, graduate school learners, and international students on University life.
